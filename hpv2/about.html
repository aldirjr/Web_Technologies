Harry Potter passion

I read the first two books in that same year and watched the 2nd movie (Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets) in the cinemas with my brother and cousin. My brother got the third book on Crhistmas, we read right away. We had to wait a few months before the forth book was released, then the fifth, but we were already in love with the saga...
By the time we were waiting for the 6th book, I already knew some English but I was making an extra effort to learn more. The reason? In Brazil, the translated version took months to be released and I didnt want to wait. I wanted to read as soon as possible, so I had to.
